YUNG FUTON


Yung Futon started her live sets less than a year ago and has been maturing her sound rapidly ever since. Inspired by the likes of Tokimonsta and Ta-Ku, Yung Futon quickly went from fiddling with her controller in the confines of her bedroom to joining the Customs crew on its Asia tour, where she made her debut at Club STOMP in Osaka, Japan. Opening for acts such as Parkgolf, Washed Out, Hayden James, and Qrion, Yung Futon has broken out onto the local electronic scene, receiving recognition for her irresistible live sets. Expect her to continue blowing up local Seattle dance floors and even bigger stages.
