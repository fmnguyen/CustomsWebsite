We admit, it's hard to define what Customs is. It's a night, but it's also a feeling and a vibe. The most important thing you should know is that we're devoted to bringing the freshest in electronic music to Seattle's dance floors. We aim to present something different each and every time, all curated by the Customs crew members, who have their ears to the ground when it comes to what's new and exciting in dance music.
